# 02 — Content and Positioning

## Professional identity

**El Houssaine Ouahad**

**Technicien Spécialisé en Intelligence Artificielle — Option Data Analyst**

## Positioning sentence

> I turn raw data into clear insights, useful dashboards, and practical digital solutions.

This sentence is preferred over generic claims such as “passionate developer” or “future AI expert”.

## Hero direction

The first screen should answer three questions immediately:

1. Who is he?
2. What does he do?
3. What can I explore next?

Suggested copy:

```text
EL HOUSSAINE OUAHAD
AI TECHNICIAN / DATA ANALYST

Turning raw data into clear direction.

[Explore selected work] [Download CV]
```

## About content

Write in first person and stay factual. Mention the official qualification, the Data Analyst specialization, the interest in practical AI, and the preference for building useful solutions. Do not call him a student in the main hero.

## Main proof

The strongest featured work is the **complete BI project**. Present it as a case study with:

- the context and business question;
- the source data and its initial problems;
- cleaning and preparation;
- KPI definitions;
- analysis and dashboard decisions;
- the final result and what it helps someone understand.

## Experience

Present YAA Innovations as practical experience involving Excel file correction and preparation, KPI work, Power BI dashboards, and data presentation. Do not invent a formal job title or numerical business impact.

## Tone

- Clear and direct.
- Specific instead of impressive-sounding.
- Human, with a little personality.
- English can be the main interface language; keep wording natural and review every sentence manually.
